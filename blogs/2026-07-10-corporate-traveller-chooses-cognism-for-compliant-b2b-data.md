Corporate Traveller, where tech and people work collectively has chosen Cognism to support its revenue teams with high-value, GDPR-compliant UK prospecting data. The company chose Cognism for its ability to tap into a real-time UK data source of prospects and deliver GDPR-compliant mobile numbers, email addresses and intent-to-buy data.
