Imagine reaching prospects the moment they’re ready to buy. With real time customer data, your team can spot key changes as they happen, such as mergers and acquisitions, job moves, funding events or team growth. That means sales teams can stay ahead, prioritise the right accounts and reach out with relevant context. So, what is real-time data, how does it work, and how can you use it without building a complex data infrastructure? In this article, we’ll explore how it works and the benefits of using accurate B2B data for real-time sales.
